Periodontal Disease Treatment in Bowie, MD

Periodontal disease progresses as the sulcus (space or pocket) between the tooth and gums gets filled with plaque, bacteria, and tartar, causing irritation to the surrounding tissue area. When these irritants remain in the space or pocket, which can cause damage to the gums and the bone area that supports the teeth!
