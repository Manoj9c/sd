Preventive Dentistry is the practice of caring for one's teeth to keep them healthy. In short, Preventive care helps to avoid dental health problems such as cavities, enamel wear, gum disease, and more.

Preventive, Restorative, and Cosmetic Dentistry Service

What Are the Benefits of Preventive Dentistry in Bowie, MD?

With good dental and oral hygiene, patients reduce their risk of getting cavities, periodontists, gingivitis, and other dental related problems. This, in turn, can help reduce the risk of secondary problems caused by poor oral health hygiene.

Bowie Dentists Recommend Treatment?

A 'Preventive Dentistry in Bowie, MD' will often recommend treatment to strengthen a tooth to make sure it does not break. For example, if a dentist sees that a tooth is weak, or cracked and in danger of breaking, dentist may advise a new filling or a dental crown or 'onlay' to protect it. This is a good way to take of your oral health than waiting until the tooth breaks, and dealing with it as an dental emergency.
